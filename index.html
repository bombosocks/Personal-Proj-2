Welcome to Old Saybrook

The Town of Old Saybrook

Although quaint it is home to an excellent array of land marks, history, great food, and shopping. Where the Connecticut River meets the Long Island Sound. It's one of the oldest towns in the state incorperated in 1845. I have lived there my whole and had the pleasure of being in their great school system and youth programs. The boat clubs and fishing will fill your time as well as the many activities in the area.
