Visiting Main Street

Old Saybrook's historic downtown has inspired many Hollywood visions of Americana. The nice sidewalks invites dog walkers, joggers, and families out for a stroll. The center of Main Street boasts many fine restaurants and shops to keep anyone happy.

Quante and Busy

The restaurant pictured here is Liv's Oyster Bar which I worked at for three years. For a high end raw-bar experience swing by Liv's. Or stop by one of the unique boutique style shops like Anchor & Compass, a gadget and leisure shop for men. Sporting items like antique beer holders and an array of tools and gadgets.
